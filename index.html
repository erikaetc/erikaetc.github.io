My adventure in NYC

From 5th of September to 29th of November, me and my fiancé will be staying in New York. He will be working at his company's NY office and I'm looking to do a "journeywoman" - visit different companies during shorter periods of time, working with them (for free), trying to learn as much as I possibly can.
My inspiration to do this and to write about it comes from Lennart Fridén/, who was inspired by Corey Haines. Lennart actually visited our company/ in beginning of July on his journeyman. As I see it, the programming part of my adventure in NYC has two ingredients; Journeywoman company visits and my own project.

- The Journeywoman

I'm looking for companies to visit during my stay in NYC, would you be able to host me?

- My own programming projects

I'm developing my own app in Java (Github / Trello ) so I'm going to work a lot on that. Part of the time I'll be sitting at home but I'm hoping to find other people in the same situation who also want to meet and code.
NYC also has a lot of meetups, so I'm going to attend a lot them to get inspired and learn even more.


Goals:

  • More knowledge and better confidence in what I know and do not know.
  • Get some practical knowledge.
  • Try new ways of working.
  • See how other companies work to get inspiration.
  • Work with some of my favourite product companies.
